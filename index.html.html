Impact of Drones:

Positive Impact of Drones

Drones in the past have shown their use in society, they have helped in emergency services, as camera technology has seen am upward spike, drones have therefore become more and far better advanced and effective at spotting things humans find hard to see, this can be seen in scenario's where drones assess the situation and look for victims in the aftermath of horrific occurances of natural disaster such as hurricanes and earthquakes, but have the advanced capabilities to prevent the disasters altogether.

Negative Impact of Drones

Given that drones are airborne, theres always a risk of collision with other flying objects, buildings and power lines, as well as animals.This could negatively impact both parties involved, causing damage or harm to property, wildlife and possibly even human life .There are also many other negative aspects to drones such as noise pollution, which can lead to discomfort and more serious health impacts on humans living close to delivery air corridors ,and negative visual impacts on urban environments.
